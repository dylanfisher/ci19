Overview

This is the website for Core Interaction, Section A. This is an introductory interactive design class taught by Nika Fisher and Dylan Fisher. All assignments, notes, readings, and other materials will be housed on this website.

The studio class meets on Tuesdays and Fridays from 9 to 11:40 am in 63 5th Avenue, Room 310.

The lab class meets on Wednesday evenings from 7 to 9:40 pm in 63 5th Avenue, Room 404.

The website is designed around the idea of staying alert, and is inspired by some of the themes presented in this class.
