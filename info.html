Overview

This is the website for Core Interaction, Section A. This is an introductory interactive design class taught by Nika Fisher and Dylan Fisher. All assignments, notes, readings, and other materials will be housed on this website.

The studio class meets on Tuesdays and Fridays from 9 to 11:40 am in 2 West 13th, Room 1011.

The lab class meets on Wednesday evenings from 7 to 9:40 pm in 2 West 13th, Room 1006.
